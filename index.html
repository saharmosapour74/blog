pasta

Pasta or kherak, meaning dough, is a general term for various types of Italian noodles, made from flour, water or egg dough . Pasta is available in about 350 different forms. Some popular forms of pasta are spaghetti, macaroni, fusilli, rigatoni, shells, capellini, penne, linguini, bucatini, tagliatelle, tortellini, ravioli, and lasagna
